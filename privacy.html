Aether Focus Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

English

Overview

Aether Focus is a local-first paid focus app for study and deep work. It helps users start focus sessions, optionally pair an NFC Anchor, and review focus history through a constellation-style progress view.

Data Stored On Device

Aether Focus may store focus settings, focus targets, focus history, active session state, and optional NFC Anchor information locally on your device.

Data Collection

Aether Focus does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use advertising trackers, and does not sell personal data. Focus history and NFC Anchor information are not collected on our servers.

Screen Time

If you use focus restrictions, app, category, or website restrictions are handled through Apple Screen Time and Family Controls features, subject to iOS permission and supported device settings.

NFC Anchor

NFC Anchor setup is optional. If you choose to pair an NFC tag, card, sticker, or compatible NFC case, Aether Focus uses the tag as a physical start or end anchor for focus sessions.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, email kamchungsitu@gmail.com.

简体中文

概述

Aether Focus 是一款本地优先的付费专注应用，面向学习、备考和深度工作场景。应用帮助用户开始专注、 可选绑定 NFC 心锚，并通过星座式进度视图回顾专注记录。

本地存储的数据

Aether Focus 可能会在你的设备本地保存专注设置、专注目标、专注历史、进行中的专注状态，以及可选的 NFC 心锚信息。

数据收集

Aether Focus 不要求注册账号，不展示广告，不使用广告追踪器，也不会出售个人数据。专注历史和 NFC 心锚信息不会被收集到我们的服务器。

屏幕使用时间

如果你使用专注限制功能，应用、类别或网站限制会通过 Apple 屏幕使用时间和 Family Controls 功能处理，并取决于 iOS 权限和设备支持情况。

NFC 心锚

NFC 心锚设置是可选功能。如果你选择绑定 NFC 标签、卡片、贴纸或兼容的 NFC 手机壳，Aether Focus 会把它作为专注开始或结束的实体锚点。

联系

如有隐私或支持问题，请发送邮件至 kamchungsitu@gmail.com。
